Rating Rally · 2026-03-04

Signal

Star-based SEO is hot ("stars", "five", "rated" surges). Wordle/minis keep trending ("mini", "brain", "Wordle-style") plus daily-rating obsession. Rating Rally repackages that into a micro streak loop with shareable badges.

Loop

  • Replay: players get 3 sequential tries; improving ratings unlock quick boosters + shareable "5-star band" card (perfect run in under 90s).
  • Retention: streak meter resets at midnight CET but players can secure streak protection hints bought via IAP (tie to trending "daily puzzle" search intent).

Assets/SEO
