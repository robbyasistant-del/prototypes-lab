Orbit Lexicon — Product Report

Date: 2026-02-28 · Genre: Word puzzle / timed strategy

1) Strategy

Hybrid of anagram and positional planning. The rotating board creates dynamic constraint, increasing mastery depth without heavy complexity. Target audience: word-game fans seeking higher skill expression than static crossword clones.

2) SEO / ASO / SEM

  • SEO: “daily word puzzle”, “anagram challenge game”, “brain word game timed”. Build organic via daily puzzle archive pages.
  • ASO: title tests Orbit Lexicon: Daily Rings / Word Orbit Challenge. Keywords: word game, brain training, anagram, daily puzzle.
  • SEM: Google App Campaign + Meta short clips showing rotating ring twist and last-second high-scoring word.

3) Core Loop

  1. Daily seeded letter orbit loads.
  2. Player composes 3+ letter words from current arrangement.
  3. Ring rotates periodically, changing opportunities.
  4. Submit valid words for points and best-word progression.
  5. End of run summary fuels immediate retry.

4) Rewards

  • Intrinsic reward: discovering longer/rarer words under pressure.
  • Extrinsic reward: daily rank percentile, streak badges, themed lexicon packs.
  • Community reward: share best word + total score card.

5) KPI Targets

  • D1 retention 38%+
  • Words submitted per run: 7+ median
  • Invalid submission rate: 20–30% (indicates experimentation)
  • D7 streak participation: > 14%
  • Share action CTR: > 6%

6) Experiments

  • Dictionary strictness: broad vs curated list (quality perception vs frustration).
  • Rotation tempo every 6s vs 8s.
  • Scoring model emphasizing rare letters vs word length.

7) Risks

  • Dictionary disputes can cause trust issues if obvious words fail.
  • Language localization complexity for non-English expansion.
  • Skill-heavy mechanics may lower conversion for very casual audience.
