Word Merge Daily — Product/Market Report (2026-02-26)

Source signals used: local market snapshot _market/data/top20-2026-02-25.json. Strong repeated demand terms across regions: daily word game, crossword, quick daily game. Top recurring IDs include word-first and daily puzzle products, supporting a short-session language concept with social streak framing.

A) Concept thesis + market rationale

Thesis: A “word merge” daily puzzle can capture users who enjoy Wordle/crossword style cognition but want faster dopamine cycles. Instead of one static answer, users can chain multiple valid words in one run, producing a stronger sense of momentum and immediate mastery.

Market rationale: daily puzzle + word mechanics appear consistently in ES/US/UK/LATAM/Europe snapshots. This suggests cross-market resilience. The prototype leans on that proven demand, then differentiates with combo chains and a compact 90-second mission.

B) SEO/ASO/SEM angle

  • Primary keywords: daily word game, word merge, word streak, quick puzzle, 90 second challenge.
  • Hooks: “Can you clear today in 90s?”, “Build 4 words before time runs out”, “Keep your streak alive”.
  • Creative concepts: split-screen timer panic + combo multiplier glow; streak flame counter; share card reveal.
  • CTR hypothesis: urgency + streak visual should outperform generic “relaxing word game” copy by +20–35% relative CTR.

C) Core loop + engagement + addiction mechanics

  1. Open app, immediate daily board with center letter constraint.
  2. Submit words, receive instant validity and combo feedback.
  3. Score accelerates with chain multiplier (variable reward).
  4. Clear objective (4 words) before timer ends.
  5. Post-win reward + social share + streak continuation.

Retention mechanics: daily reset, loss aversion via streak, and “almost there” pressure from near-goal fails.

D) Onboarding / how-to-play

Onboarding is 3 rules max: center letter required, 3+ letters, reach target before timer. This keeps first-session friction low and protects CVR from ad click to first solved puzzle.

Prototype includes dedicated help screen, explicit daily objective text, and contextual error messaging for invalid attempts.

E) Reward design

  • Daily win reward: fixed coins.
  • Streak reward: linear bonus by day to reinforce habit.
  • Cosmetic milestone: unlock visual skin at day 3+.
  • Social reward: auto-generated share line with score + streak flame.

Design intent: combine intrinsic mastery (higher combos) with extrinsic progression (coins/streak).

F) KPI targets + thresholds

  • CTR (paid creative): target 2.0–2.8%, kill if <1.2%
  • Store CVR install: target 26–34%, kill if <18%
  • D1 retention: target 34%+, kill if <25%
  • D7 retention: target 12%+, kill if <8%
  • Avg session: 3.5–5.0 min target
  • Share rate: 4–7% of DAU target

G) Experiment plan

  • A/B Hook: “90-second challenge” vs “daily streak puzzle”.
  • A/B Core: target 4 words vs 5 words.
  • A/B Reward: larger streak bonus vs cosmetic unlock emphasis.
  • Kill/keep: keep only variants with +10% D1 uplift and non-degrading session quality.

H) Risks + mitigation + next iteration

  • Risk: vocabulary frustration without strong dictionary UX. Mitigation: forgiving accepted words and hint nudge after two failures.
  • Risk: mode feels too similar to known word games. Mitigation: emphasize combo timing and merge fantasy in visuals/audio.
  • Risk: limited daily content. Mitigation: seeded generation + curated fallback boards.

Next iteration: real dictionary validator, share-card image export, and streak freeze item economy.
