Tide Loop · 2026-03-04

Market fit

“Stars”, “five”, “rated” + Wordle/Connections loops keep pointing to short bursts with rating/feedback emphasis.

The Tide Loop draws from those cues: a rhythmic puzzle that cues trending letters, includes a shareable five-star streak board, and mirrors the Only Green badge mania for social proof.

Gameplay

  • Four-beat sessions where players tap/slide to match patterns mapped to keywords like “page”, “mini”, “brain”.
  • Completing within 4 beats anchors the Hidden Tide badge; shareable image includes emoji stats and ⭐️ count.
  • Dynamic difficulty: after two perfect runs, new word/beat combos load from the Wordle #1718 dataset.

ASO/SEO

Metadata phrases: “TikTok mini puzzle streak”, “daily tide rating”, “Wordle-style hints”. Landing page highlights thumbs-up/streak share across short-form clips.

Metrics

  • D1 target: 40%. D7: 12% with #OnlyGreen share prompts.
  • Monetize via repeatable boosters + ad watch bridging between tides; add new keyword-targeting variant every Monday to align with Google Trends.
  • Risk: rhythm loops can feel repetitive, so plan variant pack featuring new “center”/“page” words keyed to trending signals.
