Block Solitaire Rush — Product/Market Report (2026-02-26)

Market evidence from local ranking snapshot shows block puzzle and casual puzzle terms recurring across regions. This supports a placement-and-clear design with modernized daily mission framing.

A) Concept thesis + market rationale

Thesis: A compact “solitaire-like” block clearer can serve broad casual audiences because it combines tactile satisfaction (placement) with strategic depth (line timing). Wrapping this in a daily 8-round mission creates routine play and measurable completion loops.

Rationale: block puzzle IDs and casual puzzle terms persist in regional tops. This concept aims to capture puzzle players who prefer visual patterns over language gameplay.

B) SEO/ASO/SEM angle

  • Keywords: block puzzle, block solitaire, daily block challenge, line clear combo.
  • Hooks: “Can you survive all 8 rounds?”, “One wrong placement and run collapses”, “Combo clears feel amazing”.
  • Creatives: before/after full clear animation, clutch last-life recovery, score explosion with multiplier.
  • CTR hypothesis: “survival rounds” positioning can lift intent for challenge-seeking segment vs plain relaxing messaging.

C) Core loop + engagement + addiction mechanics

  1. Receive 3 micro-shapes.
  2. Place shape on board (valid slot search in prototype).
  3. Clear rows/columns for combo growth.
  4. Advance rounds with life pressure.
  5. Daily completion reward and social brag.

Addiction mechanics: near-fail recovery, combo volatility, escalating stakes by round count.

D) Onboarding / instructions

Onboarding is visual-first: place, clear, chain. Help screen keeps rules concise. Fail states are explicit (no position / no lives), reducing confusion and improving first-session readability.

E) Reward design

  • Base daily reward for round-8 completion.
  • Perfect clear bonus to reward mastery.
  • 5-day streak chest (mid-term objective).
  • Share CTA after dramatic run finish.

This balances immediate reinforcement with delayed progression milestones.

F) KPI targets + thresholds

  • CTR target: 1.8–2.6% (kill <1.1%)
  • Install CVR: 24–32% (kill <17%)
  • D1: 32%+ (kill <23%)
  • D7: 11%+ (kill <7%)
  • Avg session: 4.0–6.5 min
  • Run completion rate (daily): 35–50%

G) Experiment plan

  • A/B round length: 6 vs 8 rounds.
  • A/B life system: 2 lives vs 3 lives.
  • A/B monetization trigger: revive offer after fail vs post-win cosmetic offer.
  • Kill criteria: low completion + low repeat-day return.

H) Risks + mitigation + next iteration

  • Risk: RNG frustration. Mitigation: weighted shape queue to avoid impossible states.
  • Risk: skill wall for casual players. Mitigation: assist mode on early rounds.
  • Risk: low uniqueness in crowded block genre. Mitigation: narrative daily contracts + collectible board skins.

Next: drag placement UX, true piece preview, deterministic daily seeds, and polished clear VFX.
