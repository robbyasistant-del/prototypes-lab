Strategy

Gamifies directional forecasting into a lightweight daily ritual. Targets users who enjoy prediction markets, business trivia, and quick decision loops.

SEO/ASO/SEM

  • SEO cluster: price prediction game, commodity quiz, market direction daily.
  • ASO copy focuses on "learn market intuition in 2 minutes/day".
  • SEM split: finance-curious audience vs trivia audience; compare CPI in CPI-driven geos.

Loop

  • Daily basket appears
  • Player predicts direction
  • Immediate reveal + streak pressure
  • End card prompts social compare

Rewards

  • Accuracy rank tiers (Bronze/Silver/Gold/Quant)
  • Weekly badges for volatility weeks
  • Optional seasonal leagues

KPIs

  • Day-1 completion >85%
  • D7 retention >18%
  • Avg predictions/session: 8+
  • Social repost CTR >3.5%

Experiments

  • Reveal after each guess vs batch reveal at end.
  • 3-lives model vs confidence-bet model.
  • Novice mode with hint labels (high volatility etc.).

Risks

  • Perceived financial advice risk; must add clear disclaimer.
  • Synthetic data can undermine trust unless clearly presented as game mode.
