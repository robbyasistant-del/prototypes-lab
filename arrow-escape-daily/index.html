Arrow Escape Daily v5

Dynamic S/E opposite sides · Fixed tiles by difficulty · Streak multiplier

How to play

  • Click arrows to rotate them.
  • S and E spawn on opposite sides each run.
  • Difficulty adds fixed (locked) tiles: Easy (sin cambios), Medium=5, Hard=7.
  • Streak gives a big score multiplier: x1.1, x1.2, x1.3... (x1+n*0.1).
