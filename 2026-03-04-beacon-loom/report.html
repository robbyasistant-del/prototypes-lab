Beacon Loom · story-weaving loop

Link drifted beacons to craft a gentle story, letting calm rewards echo like lighthouse flashes.

Strategy

  • Market Beacon Loom as a thoughtful memory ritual, leaning on narrative rewards and calm pacing.
  • Let the narrative be split into micro-episodes shared through notification cards.
  • Use glow-laden art and story microsites to capture ASO headlines like "daily memory tapestry."

SEO / ASO / SEM

  • Keywords: "memory loom", "daily beacon puzzle", "story weaver" to highlight narrative thread and calm feel.
  • Subtitle suggestion: "Connect beacons, weave stories, and let calm rewards glow each day."
  • SEM test: retarget players who watch story teasers with short loops showing beacons lighting on real photos.

Loop

  • Signal: capture arriving beacons and lock them to the story mood.
  • Loom: draw threads between beacons, balancing tension and glow.
  • Tether: finalize the arc to claim loom tokens and short story beats.
  • Glow: revisit key moments and plan tomorrow’s narrative seeds.

Rewards

  • Loom tokens that unlock new beacon palettes and story fragments.
  • Calm titles (North Watcher, Loom Artisan) shown on shareable glimpses.
  • Daily reflections that double as notification prompts encouraging return.

KPIs

  • Story completion rate ≥80% to confirm the pacing resonates.
  • Token redemption rate ≥40% to keep progression active.
  • Notification click-through ≥9% when referencing new beacon snippets.

Experiments

  • Test voiceover cues vs pure music to see how they affect calm score.
  • Experiment with letting players keep one tether for tomorrow to reduce friction.
  • Try giving glimpses of the story thread to friends to see if invites lift retention.

Risks

  • Too much narrative detail may slow loops; ensure micro-runs stay under 90 seconds.
  • If tokens feel too quiet, players will skip loom spending.
  • Notification story arcs must respect privacy; avoid referencing real user data.
